Privacy Policy

Last updated: 04 June 2026

Lovify respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, and protected when you access or use the Lovify platform.

1. Information We Collect

Artist Information

When artists apply to join Lovify, we may collect information such as:

  • Name or artist name
  • Email address
  • Profile information
  • Social media links
  • Artist onboarding submissions
  • Uploaded audio recordings and metadata

Listener Information

Listeners can stream publicly available recordings without creating an account.

We may automatically collect limited technical information when the platform is used.

2. Automatically Collected Information

We may collect technical information including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Pages visited
  • Referring websites
  • Usage and performance data

This information is used for analytics, platform improvement, performance monitoring, and security purposes.

3. Cookies

Lovify may use essential cookies and similar technologies necessary for platform functionality.

Cookies may be used for authentication, security, performance monitoring, and user preferences.

4. How We Use Information

Information collected may be used to:

  • Review artist applications
  • Moderate uploaded recordings
  • Operate and maintain the platform
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests
  • Improve platform performance
  • Detect fraud, abuse, or security issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

5. Sharing Information

Lovify does not sell personal information.

We may share information when necessary:

  • With service providers supporting platform operations
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect platform security and rights
  • In connection with copyright or legal claims

6. Third-Party Services

Lovify may use third-party providers for hosting, storage, analytics, authentication, content delivery, and related platform services.

These providers may process information only as necessary to provide their services.

7. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary for operational, legal, security, moderation, and business purposes.

Artist submissions and uploaded recordings may be retained even if they are not approved for publication.

8. Data Security

Lovify uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

No internet-based service can guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Object to certain uses of information

Requests may be submitted using the contact information below.

10. Children's Privacy

Lovify is not intended for children under the minimum age required by applicable law.

If we become aware that personal information has been collected from a child in violation of applicable law, we may remove it.

11. Changes to This Policy

Lovify may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the platform after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact
